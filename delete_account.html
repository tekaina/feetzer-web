Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. You can delete your Feetzer account and all associated data using one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Feetzer app on your device.
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top corner.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your choice. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
