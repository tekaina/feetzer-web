Delete Your Account

Developer: MTekaina  ·  feetzer.io@gmail.com

You have the right to delete your Feetzer account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains exactly what happens when you delete your account and how to do it.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

  • All your personal information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your location data and saved addresses
  • Your request history (questions, deliveries, services)
  • Your XP, coins, badges, streaks, and leaderboard entries
  • Your ratings, reviews, and reputation score
  • Your active subscriptions (no refund for unused period)

Method 1 — Delete from the App

Recommended

In-app account deletion

  1. Open the Feetzer app
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Scroll to Account section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm by entering your password
  7. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Method 2 — Request by Email

Alternative

Send a deletion request

  1. Email us at feetzer.io@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address registered to your Feetzer account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email
✉️ Send Deletion Request

What Data Is Deleted

  • ✅ Account credentials and profile information
  • ✅ Precise and approximate location data
  • ✅ All requests posted (questions, deliveries, services)
  • ✅ All replies and conversations
  • ✅ XP, coins, badges, and gamification data
  • ✅ Ratings and reputation scores
  • ✅ Push notification tokens

What May Be Retained

  • 🔒 Anonymised, aggregated statistical data (no personal identifiers) used to improve the service
  • 🔒 Transaction records required by financial regulations (Stripe), retained per legal requirements
  • 🔒 Logs required for fraud prevention, retained for a maximum of 90 days

Before You Delete

If you're having an issue with the app, consider contacting us first — we may be able to resolve it quickly. Email feetzer.io@gmail.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
