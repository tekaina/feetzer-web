🗑️ Delete Your Account

Feetzer by MTekaina  |  Updated: June 19, 2026

You have the right to permanently delete your Feetzer account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do so both from within the app and by contacting us directly.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your profile, exploration history, XP, badges, streaks, coins, friends list, chat history, saved places, and all other data will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

Method 1 — Delete from Within the App

1

Open Feetzer and go to your Profile

Tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar to open your profile screen.

2

Open Settings

Tap the ⚙️ settings icon in the top-right corner of your profile screen.

3

Scroll to Account Settings

Find and tap the "Account" or "Privacy" section within Settings.

4

Tap "Delete Account"

Confirm your identity and tap "Delete Account". You will be asked to confirm this irreversible action.

5

Deletion complete

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You will be signed out immediately.

Method 2 — Request by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Include the email address associated with your Feetzer account in your message.

📧 Email Us to Delete Account

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send a confirmation email.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile — name, email, profile photo, bio
  • Exploration history — all visited, favourited, and viewed places
  • Gamification data — XP, level, badges, coins, streaks, tasks, quests
  • Social data — friend connections, friendship requests, endorsements
  • Chat messages — all conversations in Firestore
  • Help requests — all posted and answered requests
  • Intent-to-visit records — all scheduled visit plans
  • Saved locations and home location preferences
  • Push notification tokens (FCM)
  • Ratings and reviews submitted
  • Account authentication data (Supabase)

Data Retained After Deletion

  • Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics that cannot identify you
  • Transaction records required by law (if you purchased a subscription)
  • Backup data — securely purged within 30 days of deletion request

Cancel Your Subscription First

If you have an active Feetzer premium subscription, please cancel it before deleting your account to avoid future charges. To cancel:

  • Open Google Play Store on your device
  • Go to Menu → Subscriptions
  • Find Feetzer and tap Cancel Subscription

Deleting your Feetzer account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription.
