Permissions We Use

We only request permissions that are essential to the app experience. All use is transparent and user-controlled.

📍 Location (Precise)

Used to find attractions near you, calculate walking distances, and show live community activity at nearby places. Accessed only while the app is open.

📷 Camera & Photos

Used only if you choose to upload or change your profile photo. Never accessed automatically or in the background.

🌐 Internet Access

Required to load attraction data, maps, weather conditions, AI tour plans, and your account information.

🔔 Notifications

Used to alert you about closing-soon attractions you've saved, friend activity, streak reminders, and live help request responses.
