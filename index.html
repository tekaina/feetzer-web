Why Feetzer?

Not a chatbot. Not a Google search. Real people, right around the corner.

📍

Location-Based Matching

Feetzer's core algorithm finds the best person to answer your question or fulfill your request based on their real-time proximity to your pinned location. Only people genuinely near you are matched.

Core Feature
🧠

Smart Expertise Matching

Beyond distance, Feetzer ranks helpers by their expertise, past request categories, and community reputation — so the right person, not just the closest, gets notified.

⚡

Instant Notifications

The moment you post a request, the top-matched nearby users receive a push notification. Accept, reply, or pass — the fastest and most qualified helper wins.

🗺️

Adjustable Search Radius

Pin your exact location on the map and slide the radius — from 300m to 5km. See live how many people are available in that zone before you even submit your request.

🤖

AI Instant Answers

For every request, Feetzer first provides an AI-generated answer from multiple sources (Mistral, OpenAI, Perplexity) — while simultaneously alerting the best nearby human helper.

🔒

Privacy First

Location is only accessed while the app is open. Your precise coordinates are never stored permanently or shared with other users. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

The Matching Algorithm

Distance is just the start. Feetzer finds the best match, not just the nearest.

📍 Proximity Score

Users closer to your pinned location score higher. Only users within your selected radius are considered at all.

🎓 Expertise Match

Helpers with experience in your request's category — tracked from past accepted requests — are ranked above generalists.

⭐ Trust Score

Community ratings, completed requests, and response time history all factor into a helper's trust score, which boosts their match priority.

⚡ Availability Signal

Users who are currently active in the app or have been recently active in your area are treated as more likely to respond quickly.

🗣️ Language Preference

Helpers who share your language preference are ranked higher so communication is smooth and efficient from the start.

🔔 Instant Notification

The top 3–5 matched helpers receive an instant push notification and can accept or pass on your request in real time.

Permissions We Use

We only request what the app genuinely needs. All use is transparent and user-controlled.

📍 Location (Precise)

Required for location-based matching. Used to find nearby helpers, calculate distances, and set your request pin. Accessed only while the app is open.

🔔 Notifications

Used to alert matched helpers of new requests, and to notify requesters when someone has accepted their request.

📷 Camera & Photos

Only if you choose to upload a profile photo. Never accessed automatically or in the background.

🌐 Internet Access

Required to load requests, match data, AI responses, maps, and account information in real time.
