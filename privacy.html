Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026  ·  Developer: MTekaina  ·  feetzer.io@gmail.com

Feetzer is a hyperlocal community network that connects users to the right nearby person to answer questions, make deliveries, or provide local services. Location data is central to how the app works and is handled with care, transparency, and user control.

1. Who We Are

Feetzer is developed and operated by MTekaina. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at feetzer.io@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo (if provided) collected during registration.

Precise location: Your real-time GPS coordinates are used to match you to nearby helpers or requests. Location is accessed only while the app is open (foreground access). We do not track your location in the background.

Request content: The text and category of questions, delivery requests, or service requests you post.

App activity: Request history, replies, ratings given and received, XP, coins, and completed tasks — used to personalise your feed and improve your match ranking.

Payment information: Processed via Stripe. Feetzer does not store card numbers or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to financial transactions.

Device information: Device type, OS version, and app version — used for crash reporting and compatibility.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To match your requests to the most relevant nearby user based on location, expertise, and trust score
  • To send push notifications to matched helpers when a new request is posted in their area
  • To calculate walking distances and display nearby helpers on the map
  • To maintain your reputation score, XP, and leaderboard ranking
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To improve app features and fix bugs

4. Location Data

Location is the core of Feetzer's matching system. Here is exactly how it is used:

  • Your location is read when you open the app and when you pin a request location on the map
  • Your approximate location zone (not precise coordinates) is stored in your profile to help you appear as a potential match for nearby requests
  • Precise coordinates are used in real time for matching calculations and are not permanently stored or shown to other users
  • Location data is shared with Google Maps / Google Places API to display maps and calculate distances
  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings, though this will limit core app functionality

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google (Maps, Places, Firebase): For maps, location services, push notifications, and authentication
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Supabase: Our backend database and authentication provider
  • AI providers (OpenAI, Mistral, Perplexity): Request text may be sent to generate AI-assisted answers. No personal identifiers are included in these calls
  • Other Feetzer users: Your display name, avatar, rating, and approximate distance are visible to users who are matched to your request

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Request history may be retained in anonymised, aggregated form for service improvement.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect profile information from within the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data via the app or at feetzer.io/delete_account
  • Portability: Request an export of your data by emailing us
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke location or notification permissions at any time via your device settings

For GDPR requests, email feetzer.io@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request".

8. Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections
  • Supabase row-level security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data
  • Passwords are hashed; Feetzer staff cannot access your password
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging is used for secure push notifications

9. Children

Feetzer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will notify you via the app or email of any significant changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
