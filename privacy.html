3. How We Use Your Data

  • To discover and rank nearby attractions, hidden gems, museums, parks, and local experiences in real time.
  • To generate personalised AI tour plans based on what's open, the weather, and your preferences.
  • To calculate walking distances and travel times from your current position.
  • To power the community attendance features — showing who else is going to a place today.
  • To run the gamification system — XP, levels, badges, streaks, daily tasks, and quests.
  • To enable social features — friend connections, live chat, and help requests.
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe.
  • To send push notifications about closing-soon attractions, friend activity, and streak reminders.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app stability via crash reporting.

5. Location Data — Detailed

Feetzer requests precise foreground location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION on Android). Location is accessed only while the app is open and visible on screen. We do not track your location in the background, when the app is minimised, or when it is closed.

Your coordinates are used to: (a) fetch nearby attractions within a 2.5 km radius, (b) calculate walking distances, and (c) display your approximate position on the in-app map. Raw GPS coordinates are never permanently stored on our servers — only the city/district-level area is recorded for matching purposes.

Location may optionally be shared with other Feetzer users as part of the community "Going Today" attendance feature. This sharing requires your explicit action (marking intent to visit a place). You can withdraw your intent at any time inside the app.

6. Social Features & Chat

Feetzer includes real-time chat between connected users, a live help request board visible to nearby explorers, and community attendance features. Messages are stored in Firebase Firestore and are only visible to the participants of the conversation. Help requests are visible to other users in your vicinity until you close or expire the request.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active.
  • Exploration history & gamification — retained to power personalised recommendations.
  • Chat messages — retained in Firestore until either party deletes them.
  • Location data — not permanently stored. Session-level coordinates are used and discarded.
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data via the account deletion page or by emailing feetzer.io@gmail.com. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / EEA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request erasure, restrict processing, receive a portable copy, and object to processing. Contact feetzer.io@gmail.com to exercise any right. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Feetzer is rated for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

10. Data Security

All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. Database access is protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only read and write their own records. Authentication uses secure JWT tokens managed by Supabase Auth. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification. The effective date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
