Privacy Policy

Developer: MTekaina  |  Effective Date: June 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Summary: Feetzer collects your location, account information, and usage data solely to provide local discovery features. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

Feetzer is a mobile application developed and operated by MTekaina. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at feetzer.io@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Precise Location: Your GPS coordinates, collected while the app is in use, to find nearby attractions and calculate walking distances.
  • Account Information: Your email address, display name, and profile photo (if provided) when you register or sign in.
  • Usage Data: Places you view, visit, favourite, or interact with inside the app, to personalise your discovery feed.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility improvements.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to a premium plan, payment is processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Used to send you relevant alerts about nearby events and your exploration activity.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To show you nearby attractions, hidden gems, and personalised tour plans.
  • To calculate walking distances and travel times.
  • To power your gamification profile — XP, badges, and streaks.
  • To improve AI-powered recommendations over time.
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe.
  • To send push notifications about time-sensitive local events.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app stability.

4. Third-Party Services

Feetzer integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Supabase – User authentication and database storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google) – Crash analytics and Firestore data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API – Attraction data and place details. Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe – Payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • OneSignal – Push notifications. OneSignal Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo – Weather data (no personal data shared). Open-Meteo
  • Mistral AI / OpenAI – AI-generated tour narratives (no personal data shared). Content is generated from attraction metadata only.

5. Location Data

Feetzer requests precise location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) to power its core discovery features. Location is accessed only while the app is in use (foreground). We do not track your location in the background or when the app is closed. Location coordinates are used exclusively to fetch nearby attractions and are not stored permanently on our servers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Usage and interaction data (visited places, favourites) is retained to improve your personalised feed. You can request deletion of your data at any time by visiting our account deletion page or emailing feetzer.io@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at feetzer.io@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Feetzer is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies, and secure authentication tokens. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
